# Concierge MVP Experiment Tracking

This document tracks the progress and results of the Concierge MVP experiment with five early-stage startup teams.

## **Success Signals**

*   **Adoption:** At least **three of the five** teams voluntarily continue to use the template and run the process *without our direct facilitation* after the 6-week engagement.
*   **Perceived Value:** The average score to the question "On a scale of 1-10, how devastating would it be if you could no longer use this process?" is **8/10 or higher**.
